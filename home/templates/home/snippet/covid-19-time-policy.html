Temporary time commitment policy
May to August 2020 interns

The Outreachy organizers have adopted a temporary time commitment policy for the May to August 2020 interns.

All interns will be required to work for 12 out of 13 weeks during the internship.

Some interns have a reduced minimum internship time commitment. Please see the table below and read our COVID-19 blog post for more details.

Who Minimum time commitment Duration
Students with school term dates changed due to COVID-19 impacts 10 hours a week During classes and exams
Anyone impacted by COVID-19 30 hours a week The whole internship period
All other interns 40 hours a week The whole internship period
