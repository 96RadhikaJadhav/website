A FOSS community can be a smaller set of contributors that all work on one piece of software or one free culture project. A community can also be comprised of many different teams that work on separate subsystems, modules, applications, libraries, tools, documentation, user experience, graphical design, and more.

Each team can submit one or more intern project proposals that their FOSS community will fund. Outreachy cannot accept intern project proposals that don't have an associated community.
