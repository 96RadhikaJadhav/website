Call for Participation in Outreachy

Outreachy provides three-month internships for people from groups traditionally underrepresented in tech. Internships run twice a year.

Outreachy welcomes participation from FOSS communities and people seeking to help out as Outreachy coordinators, mentors, and volunteers. Google Summer of Code mentors may also need to sign up to be a mentor if their coordinator is using Outreachy FOSS community page for GSoC project listings.
