Payments

Outreachy interns are paid $5,500 USD total for three months of work. The stipend is paid in three parts:

    {% if applicant_round %}
  • ${{ applicant_round.initialpayment }} initial stipend
  • ${{ applicant_round.midpayment }} mid-point stipend
  • ${{ applicant_round.finalpayment }} final stipend
    • {% elif intern_round %}
  • ${{ intern_round.initialpayment }} initial stipend
  • ${{ intern_round.midpayment }} mid-point stipend
  • ${{ intern_round.finalpayment }} final stipend
    • {% else %}
  • $1000 initial stipend
  • $2000 mid-point stipend
  • $2500 final stipend
    • {% endif %}

The stipend is paid after organizers receive successful feedback from the project mentor. Interns will be required to fill out tax forms (either a W8-BEN or a W-9) in order to be paid.
