2. Community coordinator

Each community must have a coordinator, who is an interface between Outreachy organizers and mentors. Read about community coordinator duties.

3. Mentors

Each community needs to have at least one mentor. The mentor must be willing to volunteer at least 5 hours a week during the internship period, and 5-10 hours per week during the application period. Read about mentor duties

4. Projects

Each mentor defines a project for their intern to work on. Outreachy does not allow applicants to define their own project. Read about what makes a good Outreachy project.

Projects must be licensed as open source. Projects must further free software, and not be tied to proprietary software.
